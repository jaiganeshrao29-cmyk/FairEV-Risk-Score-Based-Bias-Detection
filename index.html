Executive Final Report

🎯 Project Objective

To build a resilient, interactive prototype that demonstrates the lifecycle of automated decision-making—focusing specifically on the detection, measurement, and mitigation of algorithmic bias in Electric Vehicle subsidy allocation.

🔬 Methodology & Architecture

  • Data Generation: Synthetic dataset modeling demographic features.
  • Algorithmic Engine: Weighted risk-scoring mechanism.
  • Fairness Auditing: Real-time calculation of Approval Rates and Bias Gaps.
  • AI Integration (Gemini): Embedded LLM for report generation and conversational auditing.

🏁 Conclusion

Automated systems require active monitoring dashboards and dynamic mitigation tools built directly into the application layer to ensure equitable resource distribution.
